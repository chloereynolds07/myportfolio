Why did I become a developer?

I used to play video games all the time, mainly Roblox. From there on playing video games I wanted to develop my own personal game and that's how I gotten into coding. After starting to develop my own Roblox game my mother gave me a coding book on Web Development and my journey started between 2021 and 2022.

My mother gifted me a book named, “Get Coding!” for Christmas in 2021. With that book my interest in coding started there, and it gradually grew into a topic of interest when I came over to Roblox. I always wondered how did players create these games? I've spent 2023 and 2024 studying over development, so I wanted to start at the beginning. Using websites for example freeCodeCamp and codecademy for lessons, but it was difficult for me to fully understand how it all worked. Until my mother came across Mississippi Coding Academy and wanted me to go to classes there since I've been wanting to learn coding ever since I was 14. Ever since then I'm learning more in a month than ever before.

The path that I want to go as a developer is working and showcasing my progress. Not just to my family and friends but to my hometown Mississippi. Mississippi is not as advanced in technology as other states so it is our jobs as developers to influence others to make them see how coding is so much fun to learn if you put your mind to it and have patience to learn. Maybe one day I can teach others how to code or work in a company? The future awaits me in my journey!

When I'm not coding I always spend my time with my family and my cat, Boy. Why is her name Boy? She's actually my older sister's cat but she moved to Oxford so I adopted her into my home. She looks like she has a bad attitude, in reality she's the cutest cat in the world!
