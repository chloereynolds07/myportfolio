Why did I become a developer?

I used to play video games all the time mainly Roblox. From there on, with my love for video games I wanted to develop my own personal game. While preparing to develop my own Roblox game my mother supplied me with a coding book on Web Development, and my coding journey started between 2021 and 2022. My mother gifted me a book named, “Get Coding!” for Christmas in 2021. With that book my interest in coding started there, and started to get my undivided attention. I always wondered how did gamers create their own games? Using websites for example, "freeCodeCamp" and "codecademy" for lessons was great, but it was also difficult for me to fully understand how it all worked. Until my mother came across Mississippi Coding Academy, and approached me to go to classes there since I've been wanting to learn coding ever since I was young. Ever since then my knowledge of programing has expanded more than ever before.

The path that I want to go as a developer is showcasing my work. Not just to my family and friends, but to Mississippi which is my hometown. Mississippi is not as advanced in technology as other states, and by showing them that technolgy is the future. Maybe one day I can teach others how to code or work in a company? The future awaits me in my journey!

When I'm not coding I always spend my time with my family.
